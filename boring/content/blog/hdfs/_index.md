Hadoop Distributed File System (HDFS) is the foundational storage layer for the Big Data ecosystem. This series explores its internal implementation, from the NameNode's metadata management to the DataNode's block storage, and the complex interactions during reading and writing.

We will focus on **Hadoop 3.x** versions.
